## Spanish Heptatonic (Phrygian Dominant / Spanish / Jewish Scale)

**Formula**: 1 – ♭2 – 3 – 4 – 5 – ♭6 – ♭7

**E Spanish Heptatonic**: E – F – G♯ – A – B – C – D – E

This is Phrygian Dominant—already covered in detail in the Flamenco file (28) and the Jewish Nusach file (29) as Ahavah Rabbah. The notes are Phrygian (minor second above the root) with a raised third—creating the characteristic flamenco / Middle Eastern / Jewish "exotic" augmented second (F → G♯).

**Refer to files 28 and 29** for full treatment. In brief: this scale is the convergence point of flamenco, Arabic Hijaz, and Jewish Ahavah Rabbah—one of the most emotionally potent scales in the world precisely because it has been central to multiple living traditions for centuries.
