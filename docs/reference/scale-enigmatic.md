**Compositional approach**: use it for moments of maximum disorientation or surreal beauty. Very difficult to make "beautiful" in the conventional sense‚ÄĒaim instead for the beauty of the genuinely strange. Single, sparse melodic lines work best; dense use becomes noise.

**Instrument access**: fully chromatic instruments only. Many of the raised degrees require cross-fingering on recorder; the bawu's pitch bending is the most natural vehicle.
